# 🚀 How to Get Your App on TestFlight TODAY

**Current Status:** ✅ IPA built successfully, ❌ Apple blocked automated upload (24-hour limit)

**Solution:** Download IPA from Codemagic and upload manually via Transporter (5 minutes)

---

## Step 1: Download IPA from Codemagic (2 minutes)

1. **Go to Codemagic:** https://codemagic.io/apps
2. **Find:** "One Ummah" project
3. **Click:** Latest build (the one that just completed)
4. **Click:** "Artifacts" tab
5. **Download:** `App.ipa` file (or `One-Ummah-App-Store_71_artifacts.zip` and extract it)
6. **Save to:** Desktop or Downloads folder on your Mac

---

## Step 2: Upload via Apple Transporter (3 minutes)

### First Time (Install Transporter):
1. **Open App Store** on your Mac
2. **Search:** "Transporter"
3. **Download:** Transporter app (free, by Apple)
4. **Open:** Transporter app
5. **Sign in:** With your Apple Developer account

### Upload Process:
1. **Open Transporter app**
2. **Drag and drop** the `App.ipa` file into the window
3. **Click "Deliver"** button
4. **Wait 2-5 minutes** for upload (depends on internet speed)
5. **See:** "Delivered successfully" ✅

---

## Step 3: Install from TestFlight (10 minutes)

1. **Wait 5-10 minutes** for Apple to process the upload
2. **Open TestFlight app** on your iPhone
3. **Find:** "One Ummah" (or "Ummah Unity")
4. **Tap "Install"** (or "Update")
5. **Open the app** and test!

---

## Why This Works

- ✅ Automated upload limit only applies to API uploads
- ✅ Manual Transporter uploads are separate (unlimited)
- ✅ Same final result - app appears in TestFlight
- ✅ You can test TODAY instead of waiting 24 hours

---

## What to Test

Once installed on iPhone:

**Critical:**
- [ ] Sign up / Sign in works
- [ ] Create text post (appears in feed)
- [ ] Camera opens (gold design)
- [ ] Record video and post (under 10MB)
- [ ] "Use My Location" for prayer times works
- [ ] Notification toggle asks for permission

**If everything works:** You're ready for App Store submission! 🎉

**If something breaks:** Connect iPhone to Mac, open Safari → Develop → [iPhone] → One Ummah → Console to see errors
